ENLIGHTENMENT - EMPWERMENT - HUMAN SERVICE

Enlightenment

Manipadma defines Enlightenment to be the ultimate state of an individual's existence. It is a state where an individual has gained complete understanding of their life by gaining a complete insight into one's life purpose. In this state the individual is awakened to ones consciousness, responsibilities and abilities of enlightenment, empowerment and human service. This is a state that of bliss that is characterized by achieving meaningfulness of one's life purpose, a state of acceptance of pains as the means to achieve one's life purpose. Thus Enlightenment is a destination of chosen journey of responsibilities, effort and service of one's life purpose through ones life. A state of ultimate freedom from bondage through surrender, awakenings and service of one's purpose.

Empowerment

Manipadma defines Empowerment as the ultimate state of Achievement of Abilities that are in need and congruent to ones Life Purpose or Needs. MPF through centers has evolved many programs that serve special Empowerment needs for different segments, especially Women and Youth. There are also initiatives for skill development for weaker sections and entrepreneurship programs for different income groups. Manipadma pays a special focus on Women Empowerment through Income, Confidence Building and Growth opportunities. Overall Manipadma through various avenues that help individuals to evolve through Knowledge, Skills, Experience, Meditations, Insights, Understandings and Services in Physical, Emotional, Financial, Material and Spiritual dimensions offer Empowerment to Seekers.

Human service

Manipadma defines service in two categories, these are Material and Spiritual. Material Services cover Products and Services of life needs ranging from basics to upper of material dimensions like food, water, shelter, education, entertainment, fun, recreation, facilities, amenities, conveniences, luxuries, leisure and lifestyle. Products and Services of Spiritual nature include products and services of Enlightenment, Empowerment and Human Service of noble nature. Manipadma thorugh Empowerment and Enlightenment aims to enable individuals achieve their understandings and abilities of Material and Spiritual nature while it aims to offer noble services to individuals in weaker sects especially Suffering, Unprivileged and Destitute through Human Service
